My Story

My name is Denise and for a very long time I have been passionate about health and fitness. I truly believe that a person can live a happier life if he/she takes care of her body by eating well, excercising and sleeping well. Unfortunately, the constant demands of a busy life including school, work, family, kids, etc makes it much harder to achieve these things than we wished. However, rather than giving up we need to try a little every day to move in the right direction because we all strive to live a long beautiful life. We want to be those cool grandparents that can still go on long walks with their grandkids, can enjoy a glass of wine at a party and can still cook up a hearty breakfast for the family. Don't let all the clutter and stress of life hold you back from being the best, most healthy, most fit and most happy version of yourself. I hope you find this site useful as a resource to guide you as you take steps to live a better life. XX Denise
