My Story

My name is Denise and I have for a very long time been passionate about health and fitness. I truly believe that person can live a happier life if he/she takes care of her body by eating well, excercising and sleeping well. Although this sounds like a simply solution, we all know that sometimes given the demands of a busy lifestyle don't allow us to have even one of these things. However, if we want to live a long beautiful life then these things need to become a priortiy in our lives, no matter what. I hope you find my site useful to guide you through a week's worth of meals and ideas for excercises and stretches. XX Denise
